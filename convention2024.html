2024 Minnesota District Convention

The Minnesota District Convention will take place June 18-19, 2024 at Martin Luther College in New Ulm. All convention documents and info will appear on this page. Download the information page. You will not have to pay at registration. Invoices for convention fees have been sent via USPS. Registration is open.
